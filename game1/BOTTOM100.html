Anmol welcomes you to the game!

Can you guess the highest ranked items in top 100 lists? Remember, higher ranks equal higher points!


How To Play

  • 🎯 Two teams compete against each other.
  • 🔄 There are 10 rounds in total.
  • 📝 Each round, both teams get 4 guesses, alternating turns.
  • 💯 Points awarded equal the item's ranking number (Rank 100 = 100 pts, Rank 1 = 1 pt ).
  • 🏆 The team with the highest points at the end of the round wins that round.
  • ⚖️ The team with most rounds won, wins the Game.
